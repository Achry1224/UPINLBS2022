Wuhan University, WuHan, China, 18th - 19th March 2022

Welcome

The 7th Universal Positioning, Indoor Navigation and Location-Based Services International (UPINLBS 2022) will be held in Wuhan on 18th - 19th March 2022. The conference will be hosted the State Key Laboratory of Information Engineering in Surveying, Mapping and Remote Sensing, Wuhan University, and co-sponsored by the International Society of Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing (ISPRS). UPINLBS 2022 focuses on innovative solutions and advanced technologies in various topics related to positioning and navigation, and location-based services. This is the 7th conference in a series. Six previous conferences have been hold in Finland, the United States, and China.
