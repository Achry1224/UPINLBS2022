COVID-19 Statement

    Last Update
  • 30/Jan/2022

At present, the global epidemic of new coronary pneumonia is at a high level, and local epidemics have occurred one after another in many places in China. Before and after the Spring Festival, population flows such as student flow, migrant flow, and family visit flow increased, further increasing the risk of epidemic transmission. In order to effectively control and reduce the risk of the spread of the new crown pneumonia epidemic, and to scientifically and accurately prevent and control the epidemic, the Hubei Provincial Center for Disease Control and Prevention issued to the general public in the province the health tips on the nucleic acid testing measures for the new coronavirus in key populations in Hubei Province before and after the Spring Festival in 2022:

1. For those who came from a province without local epidemic within 14 days, check the 48-hour negative nucleic acid test certificate, and if they cannot provide it, carry out a nucleic acid test within 24 hours of their residence.

2. For personnel from land border port counties (cities, districts) or provinces with local epidemics within 14 days, who are in the control areas determined by the provincial epidemic prevention and control headquarters, implement "14 days of centralized isolation medical observation + 14 days of home isolation medical treatment. "Observation"; in other areas, the 48-hour nucleic acid test negative certificate must be checked, and a nucleic acid test should be carried out within 24 hours of the residence, and a 14-day community health monitoring will be carried out, and a nucleic acid test will be carried out every 7 days.

3. Patients with suspicious symptoms of COVID-19 in outpatient and emergency departments, patients in fever clinics, newly hospitalized patients and accompanying staff carry out nucleic acid testing as required.

4. Farmers (markets) markets, shopping malls, supermarkets, hotels, guesthouses, inns, postal express delivery, food management, transportation, cultural tourism, indoor entertainment, schools, childcare institutions, supervision, child welfare homes, nursing homes, nursing homes, sanitation New recruits in key places such as cleaning and labor-intensive enterprises "must be inspected when they enter."

5. Inpatients and escorts, takeaways, tour guides, sanitation cleaners, security guards, cooks, elevator operators, teachers, students, returnees from other provinces and hotel staff from other provinces, postal express, transportation, special Places (supervision places, children's welfare homes, nursing homes, nursing homes, etc.), farmers (markets) markets, labor-intensive enterprises and other workers shall be sampled once a week at a rate of not less than 10%.

6. Nucleic acid testing of personnel at high-risk positions, entry personnel, close contacts and other persons at risk of the epidemic shall be carried out in accordance with relevant national and provincial regulations.

The above nucleic acid testing measures will be implemented until March 15, 2022, and will be adjusted in time according to the epidemic situation inside and outside the province.

See more Information: https://www.hubei.gov.cn/zxjy/rdhy/202201/t20220112_3961424.shtml
