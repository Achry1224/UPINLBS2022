    Technical Exhibitions

    Industry participants are invited to join the conference's technical exhibition. The technical exhibition includes:

    1. One full conference pass for exhibitor.
    2. Advertising space for a logo on the UPINLBS webpage and other conference materials.
    3. Technical Exhibition Space at the venue (exhibition booth).

    The Exhibitor Hall will be open throughout the conference, with a dedicated exhibition time on Thursday, March 22th, from 3:40PM-6:00PM. A 6' skirted table and two chairs will be provided to each exhibitor.
