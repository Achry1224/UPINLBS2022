Paper Selection

The paper selection process is a two- step process involving peer review: based on abstract submissions, contributions were selected by the scientific committee; the authors of the selected contributions may submit a full paper for peer review. Papers successfully passing the peer review process will be included in the conference program and may be presented at the conference.

The papers actually presented orally or as a poster at the conference by one of the authors will be included in the IEEE Conference Publications Program and they will be accessible in IEEE Xplore.

Paper Submission

Authors whose abstract was accepted have been invited to submit a full paper via EasyChair.

EasyChair

Full papers should be prepared using the IEEE A4 paper template (available for Word and LaTeX on the IEEE website). They should not exceed 10 pages. Each full paper needs to be accompanied by a signed Copyright Transfer Agreement form. The form and more information can be found here. The signed form should be uploaded as a PDF attachment in Easy Chair.
