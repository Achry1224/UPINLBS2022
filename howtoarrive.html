How to Arrive

    Last Update
  • 25/Nov/2021

First of all, you have to arrive in Wuhan by plane or train, and then you can choose different ways to reach the UPINLBS2022 meeting place. Wuhan’s airport is called Tianhe Airport, and Wuhan’s railway stations include Wuhan Station, Hankou Station and Wuchang Station.

  • Taxi

    Taxi

    • You can call a taxi and tell the driver that you are going to the south gate of the Department of Informatics of Wuhan University in Guangbutun.

  • Subway

    Subway

    • The nearest subway station is Guangbutun Station located in Luo Yu Road of Line 2.

  • Bus

    Bus

    • The nearest bus station is Guangbutun Station,whitch has 510, 518, 521, 538, 59, 703, 715, 728, 804, 905, YX518 and YX703 buses.

COVID-19 Statement

The 7th edition of the International Conference on Ubiquitous Positioning,Indoor Navigation and Location-Based Services is taking place at Wuhan University ( https://www.whu.edu.cn/ ), WuHan, China. The event will take place in a hybrid format face-to-face and online. In case the sanitary situation does not allow any face-to-face event, the conference will take place completely online.
